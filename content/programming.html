Organizing my work.

I always start with the To-Do list, and my Issues list, both are core in my way to work. Since I use Notion they often come with a page describing all the work todo. Tasks are based on the Game Design Document (GDD) made by the designers or myself, and reunions with the team members.

A new thing I do is making flow-graph before diving into the code. I write UML usually after the code is done, to convert the flow-graph to it, allowing new comers to have a detailed plan (using Mermaid in Notion).

But my issue is that for now I don't do enough Unit Testing, even if I like it, the time consumption to create it is often hard to handle when the project is putting pressure. But I choose that in my current personal project and next projects in teams I will do it, in the end of the project it's a powerful feature to have.
