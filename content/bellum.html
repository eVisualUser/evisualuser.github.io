Bellum

Second year project at Bellecour Ecole.

Requirements:

  • Compatible on Android (v5.1).
  • Simulate Free to play market.
  • Completed in 3 months.
  • Collaborate with five game designers.

Pitch:

You play as Caesar while Brutus betray you, so you attack everyone. The goal is to kill everybody and reaching the end can be required.

Gameplay Mechanics:

  • Fighting with hand and distance weapons (swords, spears).
  • Few simple traps.
  • Modular levels, certain levels are composed of random rooms that change each time.

Free to play aspect:

The free to play aspect is composed of the support of skins for the character and it's weapons.

What I did:

I have done all the programming of the project.

  • Remade a 2D animation system to simplify the skin system integration and improve Game Designers workflow. Also created a tool that allow them to just follow the steps of creation without taking care of the implementation.
  • Added a fighting system with weapons having skins and class that can impact the player motor.
  • Worked on a concept of Token to manage the support of buying things as one time only but without requiring code.
  • Support of different currencies to buy in the shop.
  • Added a system to save player data.
